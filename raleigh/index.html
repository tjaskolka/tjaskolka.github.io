Purpose

The Raleigh Chamber of Commerce is here to provide a platform for our local businesses to be able to network with one another, to promote their products and services and to promote our community as a great place to be doing business.

Join

Are you a small business in the Raleigh area? Would you like to be a part of a thriving group of businesses? Find out more about the advantages of joining by clicking on the link below.

Highlighted Businesses

The Honey Do Husband

Need someone to mow the lawn, install an appliance, or repair the drywall? The Honey Do Husband will do all that and more. Run by a Triangle native and lifelong handyman.

Light is Love Candles

Did you know that soy candles burn longer and with less soot than paraffin? Light is Love makes 100% soy candles without artifical coloring which means they will blend in to any decor. Handmade in small batches, you will be sure to feel the love with which they are made.

Klaystation

Have the urge to create something but don't know where to start? Come visit Klaystation where there are a variety of guided creative activities to satisfy that urge. From pottery shaping to painting, our family-friendly facility will help you create something you will be proud to show off.
