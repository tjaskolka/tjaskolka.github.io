Target Audience for Bear Lake Community Board

The target audience for this site consists of local community members, youth to senior citizen. We will also be targeting adult visitors to the community from young families to retirees.
persona 1 - community member Persona: Third generation member of community Photo: Name: Sara Larson Job title/responsibilities:5th Grade school teacher at Pioneer Elementary school Demographics: 34 years old Married Mother of two children Bachelor's degree in Early Childhood Development Goals and tasks: Cares deeply for children which is why she chose her teaching career. She is concerned about providing a healthy environment for children and youth. She volunteers in the community during the summers at a youth camp. She encourages her children to also serve others daily. Her class does a community service project every year as a part of the curriculum. Environment: Usually on her laptop for school or personal use via cable internet. Uses email regularly and also posts on facebook. Gets ideas for school from pinterest. Uses her Android phone for connectivity when not at home or school. Quote: "Giving young people my best now means they will be equipped to lead with their best in the future."
persona 2 - retiree vacationer Persona: Retiree couple from Rock Spring, Wyoming Photo: Name: Bob & Carol Ecker Job title/responsibilities: Bob - retired Colonel from Francis E. Warren Air Force base Carol - clerk at Menard's in Cheyenne, retired Demographics: Bob: 68 years old Married Father of one son, 3 grandchildren Masters in Electrical Engineering Carol: 63 years old Married Mother of one son, 3 grandchildren Bachelor of Arts Goals and tasks: Both want to take time to enjoy their retirement by visiting nearby points of interest and will spend multiple days at a location with their camper van. Bob is an avid fisherman and Carol loves visiting local shops when she goes with him on fishing trips. They also enjoy visits from their grandchildren and will take them along on some of their trips. Environment: Bob is completely comfortable on a computer. They share a laptop which they take with them on trips, but Carol communicates more with her phone and table because Bob has the computer. They both use their G5 Android phones to keep in touch with their son and grandchildren on facebook. Quote: "Give a man a fish, he will eat for a day. Teach a man to fish and you will find bait in the refrigerator."
