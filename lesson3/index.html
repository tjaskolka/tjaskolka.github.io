Site Name

The name of the site is the Bear Lake Community Board.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information to residents and visitors of the Idaho area around Bear Lake. Some of the information provided could be about upcoming events, food, camping/lodging, outdoor activities and weather for the area.
