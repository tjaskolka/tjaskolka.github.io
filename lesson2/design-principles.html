Design Principles

Proximity

lowesfoods.com

Center-aligned text and pictures are closely arranged in their own column to suggest a group consisting of an image, a title, text and a link.

White Space

lowesfoods.com

Plenty of white space is evident here drawing the eye to the header image, title and link without distraction.

Fitt's Law

pandora.com

The music navigation buttons are very conveniently placed at the bottom of the mobile screen, with the play/pause button taking center stage.
